Best Fishing Spots in Northern Cyprus

Discover the most productive and beautiful fishing locations across KKTC

Kyrenia Harbour

Best For: Sea bream, Sea bass, Grouper

Access: Easy - Public harbor with parking

The historic harbor offers excellent fishing opportunities right in the heart of Kyrenia. Best fished early morning or evening.

Karpaz Peninsula

Best For: Tuna, Bonito, Mackerel

Access: Moderate - Remote rocky coastline

Wild and pristine coastline perfect for serious anglers. Bring plenty of water and sun protection.

Famagusta Bay

Best For: Mullet, Bream, Small sharks

Access: Easy - Sandy beaches with car access

Great for beginners and families. Calm waters and consistent fish activity throughout the year.

Lapta Coast

Best For: Sea bass, Grouper, Octopus

Access: Moderate - Rocky coastline

Popular local spot with experienced anglers. Rocky terrain provides excellent fish habitat.

Alsancak Pier

Best For: Night fishing, Sea bream

Access: Easy - Purpose-built fishing pier

Modern facility with lights for night fishing. Popular gathering spot for local fishing community.

Bafra Beach

Best For: Surf fishing, Mullet, Small bass

Access: Easy - Long sandy beach

Perfect for surf fishing techniques. Best during autumn and spring migrations.
