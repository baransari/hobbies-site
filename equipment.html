Essential Fishing Equipment for KKTC

Get the right gear for successful fishing in Northern Cyprus waters

Rods & Reels

Beginner Setup

  • 2.7m (9ft) spinning rod
  • 3000 size spinning reel
  • 15lb monofilament line
  • Price Range: €80-120

Shore Fishing Setup

  • 4.2m (14ft) surf casting rod
  • 6000-8000 size reel
  • 20lb mainline + shock leader
  • Price Range: €150-300

Rock Fishing Setup

  • 3.0m (10ft) heavy action rod
  • Multiplier or large spinning reel
  • 25-30lb line
  • Price Range: €200-400

Terminal Tackle

Essential Hooks

  • Circle hooks: Size 2, 1, 1/0, 2/0
  • J-hooks: Size 6, 4, 2
  • Strong sea hooks for big fish
  • Barbless hooks for catch & release

Weights & Sinkers

  • Pyramid sinkers: 2oz, 3oz, 4oz
  • Grip leads for surf fishing
  • Bomb weights: 1oz, 2oz
  • Split shot for light fishing

Swivels & Clips

  • Strong barrel swivels size 4-8
  • Coast lock snaps
  • Three-way swivels
  • Quick change beads

Bait & Lures

Natural Baits

  • Ragworm: Best all-round bait
  • Sardines: Fresh or frozen
  • Squid: Excellent for larger fish
  • Bread: Great for mullet
  • Mussels: Local favorite

Artificial Lures

  • Small spinners for sea bass
  • Rubber shads 4-6 inches
  • Metal jigs for deep water
  • Surface poppers for exciting fishing
  • Feather rigs for mackerel

Accessories

Safety Equipment

  • Life jacket for rock fishing
  • First aid kit
  • Non-slip fishing shoes
  • Sun hat and sunglasses
  • Mobile phone in waterproof case

Tools & Utilities

  • Sharp filleting knife
  • Pliers for hook removal
  • Landing net
  • Cooler box with ice
  • Rod holder/tripod

Comfort Items

  • Fishing chair or stool
  • Beach umbrella
  • Thermos for hot drinks
  • Waterproof tackle box
  • Head torch for night fishing
