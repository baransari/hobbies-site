Photography Tips for Anglers

Catch Documentation

Always photograph your catch quickly and handle fish gently. Use a wet cloth to support the fish.

Golden Hour

Best fishing photos are taken during sunrise or sunset. The natural lighting is perfect.

Waterproof Protection

Protect your camera/phone with waterproof cases. Salt spray can damage electronics.

Action Shots

Capture the fishing process - casting, fighting fish, and landing moments tell great stories.
