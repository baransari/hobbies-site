Fishing Calendar for Northern Cyprus

Plan your fishing trips according to seasonal patterns and fish behavior

Spring (March - May)

Weather Conditions

  • Temperature: 18-25°C
  • Water temp: 16-20°C
  • Mild winds, calm seas
  • Perfect fishing weather

Fish Activity

  • Sea Bream: Very active, spawning season
  • Sea Bass: Excellent fishing
  • Mullet: Schools moving closer to shore
  • Mackerel: Migration season begins

Recommended Techniques

Float fishing in calm bays, light tackle shore fishing, early morning sessions

Summer (June - August)

Weather Conditions

  • Temperature: 28-35°C
  • Water temp: 24-28°C
  • Hot and dry, light winds
  • Fish early morning or night

Fish Activity

  • Tuna: Peak season for larger species
  • Bonito: Active in deeper waters
  • Grouper: Around rocky areas
  • Flying Fish: Surface activity

Recommended Techniques

Night fishing, deep water trolling, dawn and dusk sessions, seek shaded areas

Autumn (September - November)

Weather Conditions

  • Temperature: 20-28°C
  • Water temp: 20-24°C
  • Perfect conditions return
  • Occasional storms

Fish Activity

  • Sea Bass: Excellent season
  • Bream: Feeding heavily before winter
  • Mackerel: Return migration
  • Squid: Night fishing productive

Recommended Techniques

All-day fishing possible, surf casting effective, night squid fishing

Winter (December - February)

Weather Conditions

  • Temperature: 12-18°C
  • Water temp: 14-18°C
  • Stormy weather, rough seas
  • Limited fishing days

Fish Activity

  • Grouper: Deep water fishing
  • Bream: Harbor and bay fishing
  • Whiting: Cold water specialist
  • Conger Eel: Night fishing in harbors

Recommended Techniques

Harbor fishing, sheltered bay fishing, heavy tackle for rough conditions
