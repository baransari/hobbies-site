Proven Fishing Techniques for KKTC Waters

Master the art of fishing in Mediterranean conditions

Shore Fishing (Surf Casting)

Best Locations: Bafra Beach, Famagusta Coast

Target Fish: Sea bream, Mullet, Bass

Setup:

  • 12-15ft surf rod for long casts
  • Fixed spool reel with 15-20lb line
  • Pyramid or grip sinkers (2-4oz)
  • Circle hooks size 2-6

Technique:

Cast beyond the breakers into deeper water. Use fresh ragworm or small fish as bait. Fish at dawn or dusk for best results.

Rock Fishing

Best Locations: Karpaz Peninsula, Lapta Coast

Target Fish: Grouper, Sea bass, Octopus

Setup:

  • 8-10ft medium-heavy rod
  • Multiplier reel or large fixed spool
  • 20-30lb mainline
  • Strong hooks size 1-4/0

Technique:

Fish close to rocks using live or fresh bait. Keep line tight and be ready for sudden runs. Safety first - always fish with a partner.

Night Fishing

Best Locations: Kyrenia Harbor, Alsancak Pier

Target Fish: Bream, Bass, Conger eel

Setup:

  • Sensitive tip rod for bite detection
  • LED rod tip lights
  • Fresh bait - squid, sardines
  • Multiple rods for different depths

Technique:

Fish from 8PM to 4AM when fish are most active. Use lights sparingly to avoid spooking fish. Always carry a headlamp and safety equipment.

Float Fishing

Best Locations: Harbors, Calm bays

Target Fish: Small bream, Mullet, Garfish

Setup:

  • Light float rod 10-12ft
  • Small fixed spool reel
  • Sliding float rig
  • Small hooks size 8-12

Technique:

Perfect for calm conditions and beginners. Use bread, worms, or small pieces of fish. Great for teaching children to fish.

Wind Direction

Onshore winds bring food closer to shore. Light offshore winds are ideal for casting.

Tidal Knowledge

Fish 2 hours before and after tide changes for maximum activity.

Fresh Bait

Always use the freshest bait possible. Local shops in Kyrenia sell quality fishing bait.
